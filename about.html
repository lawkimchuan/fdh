About us

Eat to your health at Fung Ding Hung

Inspired by Virgil's famous quote that "The Greatest Wealth is Health", FUNG DING HUNG Restaurant offers a selection of sumptuous Chinese nutritional recipes ranging from outstanding double-boiled soups, unique dishes tailored towards good health, to a wide choice of live seafood including fish, crab, crayfish, frogs and turtles.

In keeping with the restaurant focus on health and wellness, all dishes are prepared with NO artificial flavorings (monosodium glutamate).

Together with the inviting interior and extremely comfortable seating, FUNG DING HUNG Restaurant offers a relaxing place to eat healthy and unwind, and which will keep you coming back for more.
